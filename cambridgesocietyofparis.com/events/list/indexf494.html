In 2004\, she was appointed the first female Lord of Appeal in Ordinary in the House of Lords. She served as Law Lord until 2009\, when she became vice-president of the Supreme Court. In her early years\, Lady Hale worked in academia and became a professor of law at the University of Manchester. \nShe gained international attention in 2019 when she ruled on behalf of the Supreme Court that Boris Johnson’s prorogation of the British Parliament was illegal.\n\n\n\n\n\n\nThe Baroness will talk on the subject of “Women in the Justice System”.\n\n\n\n\n\nProgramme of the Evening
